Projects

Short summary of the projects I have worked on throughout my career

Project Analysis

I had the privilege of engaging in an NBA playoff statistics analysis, employing R code within the R Studio environment. Our analytical endeavors encompassed a multivariate analysis aimed at discerning the key variables that contribute to a player's excellence. The objective was to elucidate the interrelationships among these variables, thereby identifying the factors that define the optimal performance of players in the context of the NBA playoffs.

Aim2Flourish

I assumed a pivotal role within AIM2Flourish, undertaking comprehensive research and interviews to showcase enterprises harmonizing with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). I authored compelling narratives elucidating the nexus between business prosperity and sustainable development, accentuating the positive repercussions on both societal and environmental facets. Through proficient communication on the AIM2Flourish platform, I not only served to inspire and educate my peers but also played an indispensable role in recognizing businesses actively contributing to positive societal and environmental transformations.

Campus Dash

With the help of more colleagues, I was able to develop Campus Dash. This is a food delivery company operated by students from the University of Navarra. Our idea was to be able to compete against companies in markets like the delivery company Glovo. Our advantage comes when creating this project.  We started from the market base that Glovo itself contains. Currently, this project is in a launching stage.
