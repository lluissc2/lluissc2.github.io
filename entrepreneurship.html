Digital Competences Learnings

Harvard Course Learnings

Having the opportunity to access Harvard University courses on Innovation and Creativity, as well as Innovation and Implementation through the University of Navarra, has been an enriching experience.
Learning the course on Innovation and Creativity allowed me to better appreciate the creative process and the means to foster innovation. It has provided me with new ways to generate ideas and also instructed me on how to harness the potential of creativity in solving challenges.
It has helped me learn techniques to overcome application barriers and also how to effectively manage innovation projects. The courses have provided me with useful information and knowledge to use in the day to day life of any entrepreneur.

Google Sites Learnings

Collaborating on the team's Google Sites has been a task that we have had to insist on a lot, but in the end it has been satisfactory. Not only because we have been able to coordinate among all of us to distribute tasks but also to find solutions to the various conflicts that have arisen within the team. Ai, Glovo, artificial intelligence, the elaboration of the pitch for the "Rooster Battle" and the interview I did with Xavier Verdaguer have made us see the importance of knowing how to innovate and be different from the others. No one stands out doing what others do, you have to reinvent yourself and "risk" in a certain way. After this experience, I would advise people working with new people to emphasize communication. This skill is important to gradually progress through the project and not hit any roadblocks. Communication helps to solve problems immediately and prevents any additional ones from occurring.
